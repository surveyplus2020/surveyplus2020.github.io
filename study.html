Questionnaire sections

This questionnaire will be presented through ODK aggregate. It has six sections,

Household identification details

  • Survey phase, Area code, Cluster code?

  • Household unique ID?

  • Name of the respondent in the household?

  • Address details for the household?

  • Contact number for the household?

  • Global Positioning System (GPS)?

Demographics details

  • What is the name of the person?

  • What is his/her relationship to the head of the family?

  • What is the date of birth of the person?

  • Gender/ Marital status/ Work status?

  • How much was the last month net salary?

History of acute illness and health care utilization in past 3 months

  • Has you been ill in the last 3 months?

  • If yes,how many times in total were you ill/sick in the last 3 months?

  • When was your last time was ill within the last 3 months?

  • where you did seek health care?

  • How you did pay for these health services?

  • How much you did pay for the Consultation fee/ Medicines/surgical. etc,?

Hospital Admissions in past 12 months

  • Have you admitted in the last 12 months?

  • If yes, how many times in total were you hospitalized in the last year?

  • When were you the last time you were hospitalized?

  • Why were you admitted?

  • Where did you admit?/ How many nights did you admitted?

  • How much did pay you for the Consultation fee/Medicines/surgical. etc,?

Socio Economic Status (SES)

  • Own house (YES/NO)

  • Pucca house (high quality materials for floor, roof and walls) (YES/NO)

  • Piped water inside the house or plot (YES/NO)

  • Toilet facility inside the house or plot (YES/NO)

  • Refrigerator (YES/NO)

  • Two-wheeler (YES/NO)

Food/ Housing utilities/ Infrequent expenses

  • How often does your household buy Staple Foods/ Meat/ Diary etc.,?

  • In the last , how much did your household spend on Staple Foods/ Meat/ Diary etc.,?

  • How much did your household spend on eating outsides in Restaurents, Hotels, etc?

  • In the last month, how much did your household pay for Rent?

  • In the last month, how much did your household pay for Transportations?

  • In the last one-year, how much did your household spend on Education Fees and Supplies?
