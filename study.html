  • Stratification is the process of dividing members of the population into homogeneous subgroups.

  • Stage-1, sample of 30 clusters will be randomly selected with probability proportional sampling.

  • Stage-2, sample of 6 streets each cluster will be randomly selected with probability proportional sampling.

  • Stage-3, sample of 5 Households will be randomly selected with probability proportional sampling.

    •       Stage 3 sample selection process, example 86 households are there in the street. Total number of households in the street divided by number of households need each      street(n=5) (86/5), then we will get 17.2 (approx. 17). 7th Household is randomly selected from 1 to 17.

Questionnaire section

This questionnaire will be presented through ODK aggregate. It has six sections,

Household identification details

  • Survey phase, Area code, Cluster code?

  • Household unique ID?

  • Name of the respondent in the household?

  • Address details for the household?

  • Contact number for the household?

  • Global Positioning System (GPS)?

Demographics details

  • What is the name of the person?

  • What is his/her relationship to the head of the family?

  • What is the date of birth of the person?

  • Gender/ Marital status/ Work status?

  • How much was the last net salary?

  • Have you taking medications on a regular basis in the chronic illness?

History of acute illness and health care utilization in past 3 months

  • Has been ill in the last 3 months?

  • If yes,how many times in total was ill/sick in the last 3 months?

  • When was the last time was ill within the last 3 months?

  • where did seek health care?

  • How did pay for this health services?

  • How much did pay for Consulation fee/ Medicines/surgical. etc,?

Hospital Admissions in past 12 months

  • Have you admitted in the last 12 months?

  • If yes, how many times in total were hospitalized in the last one year?

  • When was the last time was hospitalized?

  • Why was you admitted?

  • Where did you admitted?/ How many nights did admitted?

  • How much did pay for Consulation fee/ Medicines/surgical. etc,?

Socio Economic Status (SES)

  • Own house (YES/NO)

  • Pucca house (high quality materials for floor, roof and walls) (YES/NO)

  • Piped water inside the house or plot (YES/NO)

  • Toilet facility inside the house or plot (YES/NO)

  • Refrigerator (YES/NO)

  • Two-wheeler (YES/NO)

Food/ Housing utilities/ Infrequent expenses

  • How often does your household buy Staple Foods/ Meat/ Diary etc.,?

  • In the last , how much did your household spend on Staple Foods/ Meat/ Diary etc.,?

  • How much did your household spend on eating outsides in Restaurents, Hotels, etc?

  • In the last month, how much did your household pay for Rent?

  • In the last month, how much did your household pay for Transportations?

  • In the last one-year, how much did your household spend on Education Fees and Supplies?
